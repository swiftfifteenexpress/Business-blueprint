We use Glovo On-Demand as our delivery infrastructure. You place orders manually on their business dashboard — no API or coding required at this stage.

How to Place an Order on Glovo

Step-by-step — do this after every confirmed JotForm order

1

Log into Glovo On-Demand dashboard

Go to business.glovoapp.com and log in with your Swift-Fifteen Express business account.

2

Click "New Delivery"

Select the on-demand courier option (not restaurant/food). This is for package pickup and drop-off.

3

Enter pickup details

Copy directly from Excel: pickup address, pickup contact name and phone, any special instructions (e.g. "call on arrival").

4

Enter delivery details

Copy from Excel: delivery address, recipient name and phone. Add the package description in the notes field (e.g. "2 pairs of shoes in a box — handle with care").

5

Confirm and submit the order

Review all details. Confirm. Glovo will assign a nearby rider. Update Excel: status → "Placed on Glovo".

6

Copy the tracking link the moment a rider is assigned

Glovo generates a live tracking link once a rider accepts. Copy it immediately. Paste into Excel column X. Update status → "Rider Assigned".

7

Send tracking link to client on WhatsApp

Message: "Hi [Name]! Your Swift-Fifteen rider is on the way. Track your package live here: [tracking link]. Expected delivery: [time]." Update Excel status → "In Transit".

8

Confirm delivery and close the order

When Glovo confirms delivery, update Excel status → "Delivered". Send client a WhatsApp confirmation and ask for a Google review 24 hours later.
